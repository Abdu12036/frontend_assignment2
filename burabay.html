About Burabay

Nestled in northern Kazakhstan, Burabay National Park is a natural wonder filled with crystal-clear lakes, lush pine forests, and dramatic rock formations. Often called the “Kazakh Switzerland,” it’s a perfect escape from city life. A true oasis in the midst of the Kazakh steppe, this beautiful place is located within the territory of Burabay Park. The resort attracts visitors with its pristine nature, pure air, lakes, picturesque mountain peaks, and the lush greenery of coniferous forests.

Itinerary

(1-Day Trip)

  • Morning: Departure from Nur-Sultan (3 hours by bus).
  • Arrival: Visit Burabay Lake and surrounding forest trails.
  • Lunch: Traditional Kazakh food in a local restaurant.
  • Afternoon: Hike to Okzhetpes and Zhumbaktas Rocks.
  • Evening: Return to Nur-Sultan.

Optional: 2-day program with overnight stay in a resort or guesthouse.
